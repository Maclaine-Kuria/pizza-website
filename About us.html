WELCOME TO PITCH PIZZA

At PITCH PIZZA, we are passionate about pizza. Our story is one of tradition, flavor, and community. We're not just a pizza restaurant; we're a place where families and friends come together to enjoy delicious food and create lasting memories.




OUR STORY

Our journey began in the year 2000 when our founder, Leonardo Demo, had a vision of bringing authentic, mouthwatering Italian pizza to Rome. Leonardo Dimo learned the art of pizza-making from their Italian grandmother, whose secret recipes and techniques have been passed down through generations. With these treasured family recipes and a commitment to using only the finest ingredients, PITCH PIZZA was born.




OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Pitch Pizza, we believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients. That's why we source the freshest vegetables, the finest meats, and the highest quality cheeses to create pizzas that are a cut above the rest. Our dough is made daily, and we take the time to hand-stretch it to perfection. We believe in the simple joy of a perfectly baked pizza, and we think you'll taste the difference in every bite.




COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT

We are proud to be a part of the Nairobi community. Giving back is important to us, and we regularly support local charities and events. We believe that good food has the power to bring people together, and we're committed to being a positive force in our community.




YOUR EXPERIENCE

When you dine at PITCH PIZZA, you're not just enjoying a meal; you're becoming a part of our story. Whether you're dining in with family, grabbing a slice on the go, or ordering delivery, we strive to make your experience exceptional. Thank you for choosing PITCH PIZZA. We look forward to serving you and sharing the love of pizza with you and your loved ones.
